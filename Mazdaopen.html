MAZDA NEWEST SHOWROOM OFFICIALLY OPENED ALONG HUN SEN BOULEVARD.

2023-11-06
The wait is over. HGB AUTO Co., Ltd., the sole-official distributor of Mazda cars in Cambodia officially opens its third innovative showroom. The latest showroom was built in over 3,600 square meters area. On 6th of November this year, its officially operating to expand and accommodate all Mazda brand enthusiast. It's the third-and-largest Mazda showroom built along the widest road-so called 60M road, also known as Hun Sen Boulevard. The said showroom was featured of eco-friendly & nature concept design, including leisure areas for customers of all ages: such as kid's zone, a modern cinema and a classic music lounge where you can openly play your piano piece. Additionally, a relaxing message chair and Mazda's merchandise and souvenir are also available on ground area.
The newest branch offers the full range of models, including the latest lanched models such as: The first-ever Mazda CX-60, the luxurious Mazda CX-9 and the sporty-look Mazda2.
Mazda Cambodia Mean Chey is located at Hun Sen Boulevard (60M), Sangkat Chak Angrae Kroam, Khan Mena Chey, 120602 Phnom Penh, Cambodia.
