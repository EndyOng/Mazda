MISSION

The mission of the company is to accomplish the vision to be the leading retail and wholesale distributor in Cambodia.

The company is cost-efficient, offers the best products, services, shopping environment for the local Cambodiank, the foreign expatriates and tourists.

The company also involves in Social Responsibilities by donating a certain percent of profit margins from our businesses to the community.

The company work closely with partners to promote the brand image and to help develop the market in Cambodia.

MAZDA CAMBODIA (POCHENTONG BRANCH)

Our MAZDA 4S facility features a total of 8,866 sqm of floor space and is currently the largest automotive retail facility in the country.

The 11-car showroom area offers a large range of cars inclusive of the MAZDA 2 (Sedan & Hathcback), MAZDA CX-3 (Sedan & Hatchback), MAZDA 6 (Sedan), MAZDA CX-3, MAZDA CX-5, MAZDA CX-9, MAZDA CX-30, MAZDA CX-8, and the Mazda BT-50 Thunder.

The facility features a fully equipped 10-bay aftersales service center utilizing the latest technology in computerized diagnostic tools and state-of-the-art equipment. It also boasts a well-stocked dedicated spare parts inventory to ensure quick turn around in service and repairs.

Customers are treated to a comfortable waiting lounge which features a dedicated bar, merchandise area, children’s corner, iMac internet stations, and massage lounge chairs with entertainment systems-raising the benchmark in aftersales experience.

The dealership is represented by strong management, driven sales force, and professionally trained Mazda after-sales team.

The facility is located at Russian Blvd #379, Sangkat Kakab, Khan PorSenchey.

MAZDA CAMBODIA (MEAN CHEY BRANCH)

The wait is over. HGB AUTO Co., Ltd., the sole-official distributor of Mazda cars in Cambodia officially opens its third innovative showroom. The latest showroom was built in over 3,600 square meters area. On 6th of November this year, its officially operating to expand and accommodate all Mazda brand enthusiast. It's the third-and-largest Mazda showroom built along the widest road — so called 60M road, also known as Hun Sen Boulevard. The said showroom was featured of eco-friendly & nature concept design, including leisure areas for customers of all ages: such as kid's zone, a modern cinema and a classic music lounge where you can openly play your piano piece. Additionally, a relaxing massage chair and Mazda's merchandise and souvenir are also available on ground area.

The newest branch offers the full range of models, including the latest launched models such as: The first-ever Mazda CX-60, the luxurious Mazda CX-9 and the sporty-look Mazda2.

Mazda Cambodia Mean Chey is located at Hun Sen Boulevard (60M), Sangkat Chak Angrae Kraom, Khan Mean Chey, 120602 Phnom Penh, Cambodia.
