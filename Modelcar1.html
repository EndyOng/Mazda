WELL CONNECTED

MAZDA developed MZD CONNECT to provide versatile connectivity while further enhancing safety. MZD CONNECT offers a huge range of infotainment options from the Internet through AhaTM by HARUMAN when connected to a smartphone via Bluetooth. The system's Audio feature allows selection of various music sources including AM/FM radio, CDs and mobile audio players, and Aha Internet Radio. The Communication feature reads SMS message aloud, as well as other Internet social networking services (Twitter and Facebook for example) available via Aha.

MAINTAINING YOUR FOCUS ON THE ROAD, AND ON ENJOYABLE DRIVING

The cockpit is designed according to Mazda's advanced Human-Machine Interface(HMI) concept, allowing the presentation of a wide range of information without taking attention away from the road. Building on this safety-oriented HMI design, New Mazda2 also offers the latest in connectivity functions to enable sharing of information and experiences with the outside the car. Front and rear occupants enjoy a comfortable, roomy cabin whose space belies the body's compact proportions, with a refinement that surpasses the class.
